About Saylani

Saylani Welfare International Trust is a non-profit organization founded with the mission to serve humanity and create positive change in communities across Pakistan and beyond.

Since our inception, we have been committed to providing quality education, healthcare services, food distribution, and various welfare programs to those in need. Our comprehensive approach addresses multiple aspects of community development.

Education
